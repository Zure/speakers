# Application Shell Specification

## Overview

The application shell provides a bold, minimal navigation structure for the Zure Speakers & Sessions website. Pure black and white design with clean typography creates maximum impact while keeping focus on the speakers and sessions content.

## Navigation Structure

### Header
- **Zure Logo** (left side, links to home/speakers directory)
- **Main Navigation** (right side):
  - **Speakers** - Browse all speakers
  - **Sessions** - Explore all sessions

### Footer
- **Contact Section**:
  - Heading: "Need a speaker for your event?"
  - Description: Brief text encouraging event organizers to get in touch
  - **Contact Button/Link**: Clear call-to-action to contact Zure
  - Contact info or link to Zure.com contact page

## Layout Pattern

- **Header**: Fixed top navigation bar with black background and white text
- **Main Content Area**: Full-width, clean white background
- **Footer**: Black background with white text, matches header for visual balance

## Design Notes

- Pure black (`#000000`) and white (`#FFFFFF`) throughout
- Bold typography (Space Grotesk for headings)
- Minimal chrome - content is the hero
- High contrast for maximum readability
- Mobile responsive with hamburger menu for small screens

## Navigation Items

- **Speakers** - Links to `/speakers` - Browse all Zure speakers
- **Sessions** - Links to `/sessions` - Explore available sessions
