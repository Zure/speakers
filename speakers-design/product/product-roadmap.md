# Product Roadmap

This roadmap breaks down the Zure Speakers & Sessions website into distinct sections. Each section can be designed and built independently.

## Sections

### 1. Speakers Directory
**ID:** `speakers-directory`

Browse and discover all Zure speakers. View a comprehensive list of speakers with filtering and search capabilities to find experts by topic, technology, or expertise area. Each speaker card shows key information and links to their full profile.

### 2. Sessions Catalog
**ID:** `sessions-catalog`

Explore all available sessions that Zure speakers offer. Browse sessions organized by topic or format, with search and filtering to find relevant talks. Each session card displays the title, speaker, format, and a brief description.

### 3. Speaker Profile
**ID:** `speaker-profile`

Detailed view of an individual speaker including their full bio, expertise areas, speaking experience, and a complete list of sessions they offer. Provides contact or booking information for event organizers.

### 4. Session Detail
**ID:** `session-detail`

Comprehensive view of an individual session including the full description, learning objectives, target audience, format, duration, and related sessions. Shows the speaker who delivers this session with a link to their profile.

## Notes

- **View-only**: All sections are read-only. No authentication or data management features needed.
- **Data Source**: All data is generated by an automation script from an existing system.
- **Public Access**: The entire site is publicly accessible without login requirements.
