# Product Overview

## Product Name
Zure Speakers & Sessions

## Description
A public-facing, view-only website that showcases Zure's speakers and their sessions, helping event organizers, conference planners, and companies discover engaging speakers and topics for their events. Data is automatically generated from an existing systemâ€”no authentication or manual data entry required.

## Problems We're Solving

### For Event Organizers & Companies
- **Hard to discover available speakers**: Event organizers struggle to find qualified speakers on specific topics without personal connections or lengthy research
- **No centralized view of expertise**: Companies looking for speakers have to piece together information from LinkedIn, personal websites, and word-of-mouth
- **Unclear session offerings**: Even when finding a speaker, it's unclear what topics they cover and what sessions they offer

### For Zure
- **Hidden talent**: Zure's expert consultants and their speaking expertise isn't visible to the market
- **Missed opportunities**: Potential speaking engagements are lost because event organizers don't know Zure has experts on their needed topics
- **No showcase platform**: There's no unified way to promote Zure's thought leadership and expertise

## Key Features

### Speaker Discovery
- Browse all Zure speakers with their profiles and expertise areas
- Filter and search speakers by topic, technology, or specialty
- View speaker bios, experience, and speaking history

### Session Catalog
- Explore all available sessions organized by topic or speaker
- Detailed session descriptions including format, duration, and key takeaways
- Clear indication of what audiences would benefit from each session

### Search & Filter
- Find sessions by keyword, topic, or technology
- Filter by session format (keynote, workshop, lightning talk, etc.)
- Discover speakers by expertise area or industry experience

### Speaker Profiles
- Individual speaker pages with full bio and background
- List of all sessions each speaker offers
- Contact or booking information for each speaker

### Session Details
- Comprehensive session descriptions with learning objectives
- Target audience and prerequisites
- Session format and typical duration
- Related sessions and speakers
